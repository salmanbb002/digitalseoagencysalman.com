## Useful patterns found

- Low-cost SEO sites lean into pricing, guarantees, no contracts, long service lists, and direct phone/email CTAs.
- Stronger agency pages use a clearer structure: hero, proof, framework, case study style blocks, process, services, and contact CTA.
- Local SEO competitors emphasize ranking proof, Google Business Profile, map pack visibility, city/service pages, and monthly reporting.
- AI SEO competitors now mention AEO, GEO, LLM visibility, AI answer engines, structured content, schema, and entity clarity.
- Tool-focused sites such as website crawler pages make the value proposition concrete with audit language and clear problem/solution framing.
- Copywriter/SEO strategy positioning works best when it explains business outcomes, not only deliverables.

## Design choices used

- Built a 21st.dev-inspired modern landing page pattern: hero, feature/services grid, proof section, process timeline, pricing cards, and a final CTA form.
- Used GitHub/static-site friendly architecture: plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with no build step and no external runtime dependency.
- Avoided copying competitor text or layout. The site uses the observed strategy patterns while creating original copy, visual hierarchy, and components.
- Included a project-bound generated hero image so the first viewport has a real visual asset instead of a generic gradient.

## Differentiation for digitalseoagencysalman.com

- Positioning: technical SEO plus local SEO plus AI search visibility.
- Tone: practical, transparent, and measurable rather than hype-heavy.
- Conversion path: free audit CTA appears in the navigation, hero, proof, pricing, and final form.
- SEO foundation: semantic HTML, metadata, canonical tag, Open Graph tags, and ProfessionalService schema.
